Johannes Gutenberg

The inventor of the printing press

Timeline of his life:

  • 1398- Born in Mainz, Germany; he was born to a wealthy cloth merchant.
  • 1428- Due to a power struggle he had to leave Mainz, and moved to Strasbourg. In Strasbourg he started working in the jewellery industry.
  • 1438- Working on the side in secreat he starting work on his printing technology. He sought investment from three men in Strasbourg to start his project.
  • 1440- Gutenberg introduces the project to his partners.
  • 1450- Gutenberg's printing press successfully opens. It's first test was an unknown German Poem.
  • 1452- The Bible becomes his first book to mass print due to it being a highly profitable venture. He also had another press that printed textbook's in Latin.
  • 1455180 copies of the Bible have been made with the name of the Gutenberg Bible. The project however wasn't as big of a success as he thought.
  • 1456- His main financial backer Johann Fust sues him for misappropriation of funds and win's the case. Which happens to bankrupt Gutenberg
  • 1468- Gutenberg dies in Mainz, Germany a poor man. He was never able to truly profit from his invention or get the recognition he deserved in life.
